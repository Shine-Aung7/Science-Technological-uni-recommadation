About University

Computer University(Taungoo)(Burmese: ကွန်ပျူတာတက္ကသိုလ်(တောင်ငူ)) is located at Taungoo, Bago Division, Myanmar. It was formerly known as Government Computer College, Taungoo. Computer University (Taung-Ngu) was first opened on 4 September 2000 as a College in Katumati Myo Thit, Taung-Ngu. On 20 January 2007, it was promoted into university level and was transferred to the campus of Technology University(Taung-Ngu)in Kanyo village on 20 April 2008.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Computer University(Taungoo) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government
